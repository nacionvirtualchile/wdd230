Important info

  • Total Population: Approximately 425,725 inhabitants (according to INE estimates for the year 2021).
  • Main Economic Activity: Mining, especially copper extraction, is the main source of income and employment in the region.
  • Unemployment Rate: Approximately 7.5% (2021 data)
  • Regional GDP: Antofagasta is one of the regions with the highest GDP per capita in Chile, largely due to mining.
  • Contribution to National GDP: The Antofagasta region contributes significantly to the national GDP due to its strong mining industry, particularly in copper production.
  • Employment in Mining: Mining is one of the largest employers in the region, although there are also opportunities in related sectors such as construction, services and commerce.
  • Main Export Products: Copper, molybdenum, and other minerals. Copper represents the majority of exports.
